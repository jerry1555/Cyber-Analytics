What is Data Protection

Data protection refers to safeguarding of our fundamental rights of privacy which is defined in international and regional laws and conventions. Protecting data and privacy rights of users is a major challenge for policy makers.

Benefits or advantages of Data Protection

Following are the benefits or advantages of Data Protection:

  1. The data protection helps to keep personal data secure and protected.
  2. It protects valuable information such as business transactions and financial statements.
  3. It increases quality of stored data as well data during transaction.
  4. It reduces risk of financial loss.
